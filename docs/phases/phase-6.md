# Phase 6: Hardware And Field Readiness

Phase 6 defines the physical device direction and validates practical use.

## Goals

- Turn the prototype into a reliable handheld device.
- Validate battery, display, buttons, camera position, and enclosure ergonomics.
- Keep the product language sleek, technical, compact, and restrained.

## Hardware Topics

Document and validate:

- Enclosure shape and material.
- Battery size and runtime.
- Charging circuit.
- Display selection.
- Button and D-pad feel.
- Camera placement and lens opening.
- USB/service access.
- Mounting and internal cable routing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The device can complete repeated scan/update sessions on battery.
- The screen is readable in expected work areas.
- Camera placement supports quick label framing.
- Controls remain comfortable during repeated quantity edits.
- Wi-Fi connectivity is reliable enough for normal LAN use.
